About the Project

Women Safety Analytics is a mini project designed to help communities report, track, and analyze incidents related to women's safety. The platform allows users to report incidents such as harassment, theft, and stalking, and provides an analytical dashboard to visualize the collected data, helping raise awareness and inform safety initiatives.

Safety Tips

🚶‍♀️ Stay Alert

Always be aware of your surroundings, especially in unfamiliar areas or at night.

📱 Share Your Location

Share live location with trusted family or friends when traveling alone.

🚨 Know Emergency Numbers

Keep important helpline numbers saved and easily accessible on your phone.

👥 Trust Your Instincts

If a situation feels unsafe, leave immediately and seek help from others nearby.

🔦 Well-Lit Routes

Prefer well-lit and populated routes over isolated shortcuts.

📝 Report Incidents

Reporting incidents helps authorities and communities respond and take action.
